Predictor FAQ

Quick answers about how Mood Manager’s mood & stress predictions work.

Mood Manager produces two estimates: a Mood Score and a Stress Level (both on a 1–10 scale). These predictions are based on the lifestyle inputs you enter (sleep, screen time, exercise, tasks, interruptions, etc.).

The app uses regression-based machine learning. Your inputs are preprocessed (numeric scaling and categorical encoding), then fed into trained regression models to estimate mood and stress.

The model was trained on a dataset sourced from Kaggle containing 2,000 rows of lifestyle factors and associated mood/stress scores. This project is a hackathon demo and is intended to show the end-to-end ML workflow.

Some features have a larger impact on the trained model than others. For example, in this dataset, exercise and pending tasks are strong predictors. Small changes in high-impact inputs can shift predictions more noticeably.
